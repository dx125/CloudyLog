01 · Foundations

What Puff is

Puff counts something everybody does and nobody tracks. One giant button, one satisfying tap, and over time an oddly fascinating picture of your gut.

The brand lives on a deliberate tension: funny on the surface, useful underneath. People install Puff as a joke, screenshot it for the group chat, and stay because the data quietly becomes interesting — and, for the IBS and FODMAP community, genuinely valuable. Every design decision should survive both readings: it must make a teenager laugh and a gastroenterology patient feel respected.

Personality

Cheeky, never crude

We joke about the act, never at the person. Wink, don't shout. If a copy line would embarrass someone showing the app to their mom, cut it.

Airy, never gross

The visual world is wind, clouds and breeze — mint, cream and sky. No brown, no smells, no toilet imagery. Ever.

Honest about data

Numbers are real, comparisons are sourced, and health language stays soft: patterns worth noticing, never diagnoses.

02 · Logo & mascot

Meet Gust

Gust is a small cloud of air with a face — the puff itself, personified. Gust is the app icon, the empty state, the notification voice and, someday, the sticker pack.

Light mode

Deep teal body on mint air.

Dark mode

Bright mint body on ink. This is also the App Store icon.

Construction rules

Gust is always built from one ellipse and three circles — never more lumps, never fewer. Eyes are plain dots (background color), the smile is a single rounded stroke, and two short double-gust lines may float beside the body. Gust has no limbs, no gradient shading, and no outline. In motion, Gust bobs gently (a 4–5 s float loop) and puffs up 8% on tap before settling.

DoScale Gust proportionally; minimum size 24 px. Keep clearspace equal to one eye-to-eye distance on all sides.
DoUse only palette colors: deep teal on light surfaces, bright mint on dark surfaces.
Don'tAdd gradients, outlines, shadows, extra facial features, or accessories (except seasonal event skins, which are a Pro cosmetic).
Don'tPlace Gust on coral or busy photographic backgrounds, rotate more than 8°, or pair with any toilet imagery.

Wordmark

The wordmark is Puff set in Baloo 2 Bold, lowercase never, tracking normal. It appears in deep teal on light and bright mint on dark. Lockup: Gust sits left of the wordmark at cap height, separated by half the word's width.

Round and buoyant

Two faces, both rounded, both free (Google Fonts): Baloo 2 for anything that speaks with the brand's voice, Nunito for everything the user reads.

05 · Shape, icons & motion

Everything floats

Radius

Cards 28, controls 18, chips fully round. Nothing in Puff has a sharp corner — if it looks like it could pop, it's right.

Icons

Outline style, 2 px rounded strokes, no fills except the active nav state. Wind, clouds and simple geometry; never noses, gas clouds or flames outside the streak.

Elevation

No drop shadows. Depth comes from the hard offset under the tap button (an 8–10 px solid "pillow") and surface color steps.

The tap

Press: button sinks onto its pillow, heavy haptic, Gust puffs +8%, a small cloud drifts up and fades, counter rolls. Total 450 ms. This is the product — tune it forever.

Ambient motion

Gust bobs on a 4.5 s loop on the home screen only. All motion honors reduced-motion settings; the tap haptic never does.

Sound

Off by default. Optional tap sounds are a cosmetic pack (Pro/IAP); the free tier ships one tasteful "pop".

Dark mode

Layout rules

The tap button is 158 px, centered, thumb-reachable one-handed; nothing tappable sits within 24 px of it except the quick tags. Quick tags apply to the last log for 10 seconds after a tap, then reset. The streak pill and the Pro marker on Duels are the only warm-colored elements on this screen. In dark mode the layout is identical — only tokens change.

08 · Accessibility & care

Quietly inclusive

Contrast

All text meets WCAG AA in both modes. The counter and button labels meet AAA. Charts never rely on color alone — the highlighted bar is also the tallest labeled element.

Touch & motor

Minimum target 44 px; the core action is a 158 px button. Full VoiceOver/TalkBack labels ("Log a toot, 14 today"). The whole core loop works with one thumb, no typing.

Motion & sound

Reduced-motion disables Gust's bobbing and the puff animation, keeping only a color pulse. Sound is opt-in, always.

Dignity

Puff may be used by people with GI conditions. Copy never shames a count, high or low, and health framing is always ranges and patterns — with a standing "this isn't medical advice" note in Stats.
